Cruising the Shannon

Cruising times and Suggested Cruises

One of the most important things to keep in mind when planning your boating holiday on the River Shannon is the time it takes to get from town to town keeping in mind the time spent passing through locks and under bridges.  A cruise on the Shannon is all about relaxation.  The boats go very slowly, you will be overtaken by cyclists and even joggers if they are fast enough, so relax and watch the beautiful scenery go by.  The chart below shows the average cruising times between towns on the River Shannon in hours.  If you are heading upriver to the north read horizontally and if you are heading south read vertically.  Keep in mind also that we can arrange one way hire with hire companies who have two or more hire bases and this will give you more time to explore everything the river and its lakes have to offer or indeed more time to relax and go slowly!
