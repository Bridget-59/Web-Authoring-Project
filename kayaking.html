Kayaking & Canoeing - Shannon Locations

My Next Adventure provides kayaking tours of Lough Derg and are situated in Killaloe, Co.Clare. All equipment is provided and groups are kept small to enable everyone ask questions and learn about this historic town and surrounding area.  Go places that bigger boats cannot go and get close to nature, in and out of the water! (My Next Adventure, 2023) My Next Adventure

Shannon River Adventure on the shore of Lough Bofin just outside the village of Rooskey offer both Kayaking and Canoeing among other pursuits.  They opened in 2009 and provide kayaking trips around the lakes in tradition cockpit kayaks as well as the sit-on-top kayaks for the inexperienced.  They cater for all ages and abilities and all activities are instructor led with a minimum of four participants. A paddle around the tranquil waters of Lough Bofin and Lough Boderg, both part of the River Shannon, is an opportunity to take it easy and enjoy the freedom of an open lake.  All equipment is provided including wetsuits, buoyancy aids, helmets and paddles.  Shannon River Adventure

The Paddle Shack offer Kayaking trips on the River Shannon and are located near Carrick-on-Shannon.  All equipment is provided and they provide both single and double kayaks.  They will take you exploring along the river in an adventure that is tailored to your group.  (The Paddle Shack, 2023) The Paddle Shack

P Mac Kayaks offer kayaking adventures on Lough Key with tailor made kayak sessions.  Paddle around the lake and venture through the streams in the woods of Lough Key Forest Park.  (Lough Key Forest and Activity Park, 2022) P Mac Kayaks

Lough Allen Adventures provide two kayaking tours a day to O'Connor's Island and the Drumshamabo.  Both tours are suitable for beginners and experienced water enthusiasts.  They also do a combination of kayaking and paddleboarding so there is something for everyone.  Lough Allen Adventure Centre is the only kayaking and paddleboarding tour provider who are permitted to bring kayaks and SUPs through the working lock gate.  Both tours promise an adventure filled trip through beautiful waters.  (Lough Allen Adventure, 2021) Lough Allen Adventure
