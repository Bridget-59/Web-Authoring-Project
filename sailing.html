Sailing Breaks

Sailing clubs, activity centres and yacht clubs offer sailing on the inland waterways, mainly in the lakes. Whether its dinghies or sailing cruisers that appeal to you the lakes on the Shannon offer superb wide open expanses of water for touring or racing.  Lake sailing offers its own challenges and hilly shorelines and islands can provide sufficient changes in wind direction to engage young and old in recreational or competitive sailing.



Killaloe Sailing Club is on the shores of Lough Derg, just outside Killaloe in Co. Clare.  It is an ISA affiliated dinghy sailing club in existence since 1963.  The club which currently has in excess of 300 members is open to anyone who is interested in learning to sail or improve their sailing abilities.  The club are an Irish Sailing Accredited Training School since 2017 and hold summer camps for children and regular coaching sessions for both children and adults at weekends. Killaloe Sailing Club



Lough Derg Yacht Club was founded in 1835 and is situated in Dromineer.  Day membership is available to visitors to the club and sailing classes are available to non-members.  The club offers great sailing and cruising for all types of boats including Optimist, Mirrors, Lasers, 420s and the Shannon One Design. Lough Derg Yacht Club

 



Lough Ree Yacht Club was founded in 1770 and was known at that time as Athlone Yacht Club.  It is located in Ballyglass, Coosan just north of Athlone town and is proud of its status as the second oldest yacht clubs in the world.  LYRC run training courses every year for all levels and applications for the courses are available to non-members on a first come basis as the courses are very popular.  A small number of club boats are available for hire again on a first come basis.Lough Ree Yacht Club

 

The Allen Centre is an Irish Sailing training centre on the banks of Lough Allen in Drumshambo.  This area is close to the rising of the River Shannon and is renowned for its beauty and unspoiled landscape.  Lough Allen is large enough to cater for day sailing as well as sheltered and unsheltered training and racing activities.  The centre stocks sailing dinghies including 8 Picos, 2 Fevas and a RS Venture and also has access and parking for own-boat usage.Lough Erne Yacht Club
